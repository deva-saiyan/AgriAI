Project Details

Project Overview

Abstract

Plant diseases reduce agricultural productivity and affect crop quality. This project uses Deep Learning and Computer Vision techniques to automatically detect plant leaf diseases using image analysis. CNN, MobileNetV3 Large and Fusion Models are used for accurate disease classification and real-time agricultural support.

Project Aim

  • Detect plant diseases automatically
  • Improve crop productivity
  • Reduce agricultural loss
  • Support smart farming systems
  • Provide AI-based disease prediction
Research Problem

Problem Statement

Traditional plant disease identification methods are manual, time-consuming and require expert agricultural knowledge. Farmers struggle to identify diseases accurately which leads to crop damage and productivity loss. Therefore, an intelligent Deep Learning based disease detection system is necessary.
